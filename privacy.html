Privacy Policy

This Node Operator (UNO) is committed to protecting the privacy of Unity Nodes Lease Operators (ULOs). This policy outlines how the Unity Nodes Operator (UNO) handles your personal information and device access.

info

1. Information Collection and Use

The Unity Nodes Operator (UNO) respects your privacy and is committed to minimal data collection practices. We do not access, collect, or use your personal information including:

  • Location data or GPS information
  • Personal contacts or phone records
  • Text messages or call logs
  • Photos, videos, or media files
  • Personal documents or files unrelated to the Unity Nodes device
phonelink_setup

2. Device Access and Software Updates

The UNO will only access your phone or mobile device for the following specific purposes:

  • Software Updates: To update, maintain, and optimize the Unity Nodes software and firmware installed on the leased device
  • Technical Support: To provide technical assistance, troubleshooting, and training related to the Unity Nodes device and application

Important: All device access for technical support and training purposes will only occur with your explicit approval and consent.

support_agent

3. Technical Support and Training

When providing technical support or training, the UNO may access:

  • The Unity Nodes application and its settings
  • The leased Unity Nodes hardware device
  • Network and connectivity settings related to the Unity Nodes device

This access is strictly limited to resolving issues, providing guidance, and ensuring proper operation of the Unity Nodes equipment. The UNO will not access any other applications, data, or functionality on your personal device without your explicit permission.

security

4. Data Security

The UNO implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect any data related to the Unity Nodes device and service. We are committed to:

  • Maintaining the confidentiality of any technical information accessed during support
  • Using secure methods for software updates and device maintenance
  • Limiting access to only authorized personnel for legitimate support purposes
  • Not sharing, selling, or distributing your personal information to third parties
verified_user

5. Your Rights and Control

As a Unity Nodes Lease Operator, you have the right to:

  • Request information about what device access has occurred
  • Ask questions about our privacy practices
  • Receive support through alternative methods if you prefer not to grant device access

For information about how Unity Technologies handles user privacy and data, please refer to their official privacy policy:

Unity Game Player and App User Privacy Policy

7. Questions and Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact Unity Nodes support through the official channels provided with your lease agreement.

update

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify ULOs of any material changes through appropriate channels. Your continued use of Unity Nodes services after such updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
